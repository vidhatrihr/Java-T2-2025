Call for Nominations

Secretary, Devabhāṣā: The Sanskrit Society (2025)

Namo namah,

We are inviting nominations for the post of Secretary of Devabhāṣā: The Sanskrit Society for the year 2025.

If you are passionate about Sanskrit and committed to upholding Bhāratiya cultural and knowledge traditions, this is a wonderful opportunity to lead and contribute.

Eligibility Criteria:
  • Age ≤ 25 as on 31st Dec 2025
  • CGPA ≥ 7.5
  • Not under RKA
  • No record of academic or non-academic misconduct
  • Completed ≥ 4 academic courses
  • Not held Secretary post before
  • Must remain enrolled for 3 consecutive terms (including the elected term)
  • Criteria may be waived by Student Affairs on case-by-case basis
Roles and Responsibilities:
  • Organising regular Sanskrit learning sessions
  • Ensuring posters are released on key festivals and events
  • Overseeing periodic release of the Society's magazine
  • Representing the Society in Student Affairs meetings
  • Submitting reports on Society’s activities
